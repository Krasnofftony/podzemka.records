Podzemka is...

... the only way to British sound. At least for those artists, musicians, producers, dj-s, performers and acts who are currently in Kyiv, Ukraine. What does British sound mean? Well that’s more than just a sound, to be specific. It’s a style, attitude, passion and character with a bite. It’s a sound that strikes the mind and stays there forever regardless of the genre, mood or complexity of the track. It may even fool you by sounding harsh and cheap but only because it was styled, designed and intended to be played that way. Would it be a hit? No guarantees on that one though. As a team of top professionals with international experience under the belt (find more in the articles below) we know exactly what it takes to record a major hit . You’d be surprised that it usually takes an effort of a group of 20 and beyond professionals in songwriting, composition, recording, production, marketing, management, advertising and distribution with a budget exceeding 5, 000 USD on top to record a commercial hit. We honestly can’t guarantee you the same result unless you have the same resources. What we can offer you instead is to discover your unique style and sound and to seal it on the record FOREVER. The record with quality no worse than of any radio hit ever streamed. And we strongly believe that it is enough to finally make you feel complete as an artist, musician, producer, dj, performer or act. If that doesn’t sound appealing, we have free wi-fi, coffee, tea, water and cool photo zones to stop by after all ;)
