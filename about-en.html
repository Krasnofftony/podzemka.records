Pidzemka is...

… no ordinary recording studio for a few reasons. Firstly, hardly ever we saw a music studio designed to resemble London Underground. And secondly, that’s the entire point! That was the only way to convey that your personality goes far beyond anything else. We strive to create music that stands out among the noise around. Hear us out. YOUR MUSIC HAS TO BE OUTSTANDING!

In Pidzemka we neither have customers noк take orders. We collaborate with artists to create phenomenal pieces. Together, we enjoy the ride just as much as we are proud of our releases. Moreover, once you come to Pidzemka, you are likely to end up in a whole new world of imagery and creativity with a completely lost track of time.

To help you dive right into your inner self, music and ideas, the studio is equipped with two soundproof and noise-absorbant rooms. A smaller one is most commonly used for recording vocals and therefore is referred to as a Vocal Booth. Another room (Control Room) is larger and staffed with all the gear imaginable for professional recording, mixing and practice. Feel free to check out our tech riders here.

In order to make your experience nearly perfect as much as a trip with Pidzemka special, we have two guides on board. is a sound guy. delivers marketing and PR. Both have an impressive background with underground rock bands as well as mainstream pop artists on the bill. Such a tremendous diversity has the greatest value in Pidzemka since it’s the secret ingredient that allows to turn a regular release into a fascinating event of your life. Feel free to check out experience of our guides in the section to the right. Meanwhile, we also insist you take a listen to our portfolio under the link.

With that in view, it seams reasonable enough to say that Pidzemka is no ordinary studio in Kyiv. First and foremost, it’s a path to your inner creativity and authentic self-expression through the music. Because your identity stands out. Your music is outstanding!

Team:

Bohdan | Sound Guide

Regardless of your skills, there’s always a moment when you catch yourself thinking “I’ve tried it all, yet there is no bite to the track yet”. And that’s exactly the moment you should approach Bohdan. Bohdan has been involved into sound crafting for over 10 years by now. He is capable of mixing live shows, mastering studio tapes or even run a field recording. That’s not it however. He can also write arrangements equally well for a rock band as well as ambient soundtracks for a motion picture. That’s why we are certain that you’d be able to hit it off right from the start.
