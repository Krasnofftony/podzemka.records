Audio Production at Podzemka Records Studio

Recording Studio

Recording studio Podzemka Records consists of two interconnected soundproof rooms with a full noise absorption. These are a vocal booth (2 sq.m.) and a control room (6 sq.m.) with an 8 XLR input multicore cable in between them. Such configuration would allow to record equally well a singer (or a group of singers) singing to a prerecorded beat as well as a band playing together live with drums recorded in a separate booth simultaneously. A TV screen mounted on the wall of the vocal booth would conveniently replace a music stand with lyrics and score as much as it would tremendously facilitate the process of recording voice-overs to a video.

Tech rider:

  • Mic: Rode NT1-A
  • Headphones: Beyerdynamic dt 770 Pro
  • Audio Interface: Universal Audio Apollo Twin

Rehearsal Space

Podzemka is a great spot for rehearsals too. It could work for a small act (up to 4 pieces band). Or for a DJ who needs to test a track on big speakers prior to the release. Or for a music tutor with a student. Even if you’re all by yourself willing to lock yourself down in the vocal booth and have some fun while shouting, screaming and yelling please know: you’re very welcomed here too (unless you start destroying the gear). Moreover, if you have your own recording gear and you don’t need support from our tech guy - what’s the point of overpaying, right? Simply book a room for a rehearsal - and there you go, bud! ;)

Tech rider:

  • Mics: Shure SM58, S55
  • Music station Akai MPC X: 2 combined inputs, 2 line inputs (2 guitars/2 vocals or 3 guitars/1 vocal or 4 guitars), 8 Midi inputs (for keys, synth and drums)
  • Electromotive Laboratories Model 2 active monitors
  • JBL EON Compact floor monitor

Balancing and EQ are entirely digital.
