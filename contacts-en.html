How to find us

Podzemka is located few blocks away from Silver Breeze mall and a ramp coming from Paton bridge. The nearest underground station is Livoberezhna, which is 30 mins away of walking distance. In addition, there’s a bus stop right next to Podzemka called “Avtomagazin”. It is served with buses #49 and #108. When arrived, proceed to the arch on the right to the street address sign (follow the red arrow on the picture). Then, turn left. You’d able to see 2 basement doors next to each other. One is blue, another is brown. Podzemka Records is behind the brown door.
