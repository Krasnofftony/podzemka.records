Recording Studio

Frustrated that no one else can hear the melody inside your head? Well, that’s the high time to check out Pidzemka. Pidzemka Records studio offers recording and production services of any kind at any stage. From recording vocals to an existing backing track (Vocal Recording) to editing, mixing and mastering a multitrack (Mixing and Mastering) to a full production of a song from scratch (Full Production). Don’t be shy! Share your thoughts, ideas and expectations with us and let’s rock! We promise to take the best care possible of your demo to make it shine and you proud. Just shoot us a line HERE

Rehearsal Space

Podzemka is a great spot for rehearsals too. It could work for a small act (up to 4 pieces band). Or for a DJ who needs to test a track on big speakers prior to the release. Or for a music tutor with a student. Even if you’re all by yourself willing to lock yourself down in the vocal booth and have some fun while shouting, screaming and yelling please know: you’re very welcomed here too (unless you start destroying the gear). Moreover, if you have your own recording gear and you don’t need support from our tech guy - what’s the point of overpaying, right? Simply book a room for a rehearsal - and there you go, bud! ;)
